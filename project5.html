Having A Healthy Diet

Importance of a healthy diet

A healthy diet is all about balance and variety. It emphasizes whole foods like fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats, while limiting processed items high in sugar, salt, and unhealthy oils. Eating colorful produce ensures a wide range of vitamins and antioxidants, while proteins support muscle repair and growth. Whole grains provide steady energy, and good fats from sources like nuts or olive oil help heart health.

Nutrition Tips

Nutrition is the science of how food nourishes the body and supports overall health. It focuses on the intake of essential nutrients—such as carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals—that provide energy, build and repair tissues, and regulate bodily functions.
